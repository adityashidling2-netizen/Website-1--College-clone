About Government Polytechnic Hubli

Government Polytechnic College Hubli was established in year 2009 by the Government of Karnataka. Our mission is to provide quality technical education to students from all backgrounds. We are affiliated with the Directiorate of Technical Education, Bangalore and approved by AICTE. New Delhi

Our Vision

To be a leading institute in technical education, creating skilled diploma engineers for industry and society.

Our Mission

  • Build strong industry connections for student placements
