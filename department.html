Departments of Government Polytechnic Hubballi

Government Polytechnic Hubballi offers various diploma engineering courses with modern laboratories and experienced faculty members.

1. Computer Science Engineering

The Computer Science department focuses on programming, web development, database management and software skills.

  • Programming Lab
  • Web Development
  • Database Management
  • Computer Networks

2. Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering department provides knowledge about construction, building planning and surveying.

  • Survey Lab
  • Construction Materials
  • AutoCAD Basics
  • Structural Engineering

3. Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical department teaches manufacturing, machine design and workshop technologies.

  • Machine Workshop
  • Lathe Operations
  • Thermal Engineering
  • CAD/CAM Basics

4. Electronics and Communication Engineering

The E&C department focuses on electronic circuits, communication systems and embedded technologies.

  • Electronic Devices
  • Digital Electronics
  • Microcontrollers
  • Communication Systems
